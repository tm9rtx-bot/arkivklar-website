Process

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do we need to install any software?

No. The delivery runs directly in the browser. You double-click the startup file and everything is ready. No installation, no server setup, no IT dependencies.

How many documents can you handle?

There is no upper limit, it is just a matter of time. We handle everything from a few hundred to hundreds of thousands of documents. Larger volumes take a bit longer, but the quality is the same.

What does it cost?

Pricing depends on volume and complexity. We always offer a fixed price after a brief assessment, no surprises. Send us your project scope and document count and you will have a concrete proposal promptly.

What about GDPR and data security?

All processing happens locally. No data is sent to external servers or cloud services. We sign a data processing agreement on request.

Which file types are supported?

PDF, DOCX, XLSX, DWG and TXT today. We continuously add support for more formats as needed. If you have specific OCR precision requirements, for example for handwriting or technical drawings, we determine which engine is the best fit.

How long does it take?

It depends on volume and complexity. We always provide a time estimate after the initial assessment, so you know what to expect before we start.

Ready for a no-obligation consultation?

Send us your project scope and estimated document volume and we will get back to you with a concrete proposal.
